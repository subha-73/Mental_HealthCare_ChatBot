About Us

Welcome to our mental health chatbot! This mental health chatbot is designed to provide reliable information and resources to help individuals improve their mental health and wellbeing.

We understand that seeking help for mental health concerns can be challenging, and our chatbot is designed to make the process as comfortable and accessible as possible.

However, please remember that our chatbot is not a substitute for professional help. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please seek help from a mental health professional.

Our Mission

We believe that education and awareness are critical components of mental health. Our chatbot aimed to provide accurate and up-to-date information on various mental health conditions, including symptoms, causes and effects. We also provide a variety of resouces, such as mental health tests and exercises, to help you evaluate your mental health and improve your overall wellbeing.
